      │    └── pgvector (document_chunks)
      ├── Extraction Pipeline
      │    ├── ExtractionPipelineService
      │    └── GoldenSet / Evaluator
      └── Workflow (draft → published)

[PostgreSQL 16 + pgvector]
[Valkey 8 (JWT blacklist, RAG cache, rate limit)]
[Milvus (벡터 DB, 192.168.0.31:19530)]
